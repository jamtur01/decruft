This **list of films featuring [time loops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_loop "Time loop")** in which characters experience the same period of time which is repeatedly resetting: when a certain condition is met, such as a death of a character or a clock reaches a certain time, the loop starts again, with one or more characters retaining the memories from the previous loop.[^1]: 207  The list provides the names and brief synopses of films in which time loops are a prominent plot device. 

For a list of films that include any kind of time travel (including time loops) see [time travel in films](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_time_travel_works_of_fiction#Time_travel_in_films "List of time travel works of fiction").
